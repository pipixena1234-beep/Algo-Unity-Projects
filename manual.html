Computer Tricks

  • Ctrl + Shift + T — Reopen the last closed browser tab instantly.
  • Alt + Tab — Quickly switch between open apps or windows.
  • Windows + L — Instantly lock your computer screen.
  • Ctrl + Backspace — Delete entire words instead of one letter at a time.
  • Snipping Tool / Snip & Sketch — Take screenshots of any part of your screen easily.
