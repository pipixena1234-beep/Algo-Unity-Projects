Sticky Notes

  • Unity Hub — Always match project’s Unity version with the installed version in Unity Hub to avoid compatibility issues!
  • Assets — Use the Assets window in Unity to organize, import, and access all your game files—like scripts, textures, and prefabs—efficiently.
  • Inspector Component — Instantly lock your computer screen.
  • Mono Behaviour — Delete entire words instead of one letter at a time.
  • Prefabs — Take screenshots of any part of your screen easily.
